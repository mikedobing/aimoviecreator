# Visual Prompts: Act 1 - The Awakening (Auran Chronicles) - Gotham Edition

Use these prompts with **Nano Banano Pro**. These are tuned to the **Gotham-Noir** aesthetic (dark, moody London) without the green tint, featuring **Auran Blue** and **Sheol Void** highlights.

## Scene: The Church (Rainy Night)

### P01 - The Setting
> **Prompt**: [Abandoned gothic church in the rainy London night] + [Moody cinematic shot from a low angle] + [35mm lens, wide shot] + [Low-key lighting, cold blue and charcoal shadows, wet stone texture] + **[GOTHAM_NOIR]**

### P02 - The Chase (Close-up)
> **Prompt**: [Young woman breathing heavily, terrified expression] + [Close-up tracking shot, raindrops on skin] + [Fast-shutter speed for motion blur] + [Deep blue-noir shadows, no green tint] + **[GOTHAM_NOIR]**

### P03 - The Sheol Demon (Atmospheric)
> **Prompt**: [Shadowy monstrous figure emerging from church doorway] + [High-contrast silhouette shot] + [Dynamic camera pan] + [Distorted ink-like energy, dark void texture] + **[SHEOL_VOID]**

### P04 - The Transfer (The Moment)
> **Prompt**: [Dying woman reaching out to Seb's face] + [Extreme close-up on contact point] + [Shallow depth of field, candlelit flicker] + [Explosion of blinding blue-white energy from the touch] + **[AURAN_BLUE]**

### P05 - The Awakening (Eye Macro)
> **Prompt**: [Macro shot of Seb's human eye widening] + [Extreme detail on iris and pupil contracting] + [Blue bioluminescent sparks beginning to pulse within the pupil] + [Cinematic cold blue-noir lighting] + **[AURAN_BLUE]**

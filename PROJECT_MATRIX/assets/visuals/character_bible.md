# CHARACTER BIBLE: The Auran Chronicles (Project Matrix)

## 1. Seb (Protagonist)
- **Role**: The Message Bearer / The Awakened.
- **Background**: A young man pulled from his mundane life after witnessing a supernatural murder.
- **Visual Description**: 
    - **Clothing**: Low-profile urban wear, dark colors, practical but stylish. Reactive to blue bioluminescence.
    - **Key Feature**: **Auran Eyes**—at climax, his eyes glow with a deep pulsing blue energy.
- **Movement Style**: Evolving from clumsy human to superhuman agility (Magistry training).

## 2. Caleb (Mentor)
- **Role**: High Mage of the Magistry.
- **Visual Description**: 
    - **Appearance**: Elderly but physically fit, sharp eyes, weathered face.
    - **Clothing**: Layered blacks/charcoals, "Gotham-Noir" style trench coat, hidden mystical etchings.
- **Presence**: Commanding, knowledgeable, weary from the centuries-long war.

## 3. The Sheol (Antagonists)
- **Nature**: Demonic race that possesses human hosts.
- **Visual Description**: 
    - **Physical**: Monstrous, bio-mechanical, and reptilian. Sleek, dark iridescent scales, elongated "Alien-esque" skull, and multiple needle-sharp teeth.
- **Energy**: Distorted, ink-like black energy (SHEOL_VOID) venting from joints or gills.
    - **Possession**: When active, host eyes glitch with black/red energy, veins blacken.
- **Combat Style**: Brutal, unnatural movement, fast and unpredictable.

## 4. The Magistry Agent (Dying Woman)
- **Role**: Catalyst of the story.
- **Visual Description**: Clad in black leather, weary from the chase, bleeding from demonic wounds.
- **Key Action**: The Transfer—direct physical contact to pass the "Message" into Seb's mind.
